Previous Exam papers

When using exam papers (and solutions) from previous iterations of this modules you should note that

  • There may be differences in notation, or difference in emphasis on topics, or even different topics for year to year.
  • The outline solutions were most probably not written with, you the student in mind, but instead written with the external examiner in mind. So solutions are intended to indicative, may not be as clear as you may wish, and may use different (but comparable and understandable to a subject expert) notation/techniques.
