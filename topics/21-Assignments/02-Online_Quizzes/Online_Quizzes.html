Online Quizzes

We will have a series of online quizzes (hopefully) every second week. The quizzes will be available here (and on Moodle) and will be open for a limited time, as discussed in class. These quizzes will contribute towards your CA grade.

After each quiz closes a copy of the quiz, for revision purposes, will be made available. The revision quizzes will be available for the entire semester and you can retake them as often as you like.
